How to Create an Account

Creating an account on QGig is quick and easy. Follow these steps:

  1. Visit the Sign Up Page: Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation bar
  2. Choose Your Account Type:
    • Professional: If you're looking for job opportunities
    • Institution: If you're an employer looking to hire
  3. Fill in Your Details:
    • Email address (must be valid)
    • Strong password (at least 8 characters)
    • Confirm your password
  4. Agree to Terms: Read and accept our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  5. Click "Create Account": You'll be redirected to complete your profile

Tips:

  • Use a professional email address
  • Choose a strong, unique password
  • Keep your login credentials secure
