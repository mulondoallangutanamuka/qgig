How to Create an Account

Creating an account on QGig is quick and easy:

  1. Visit Sign Up: Click "Sign Up" in the navigation bar
  2. Choose Account Type:
    • Professional: For job seekers
    • Institution: For employers
  3. Enter Details: Email, password, and confirm password
  4. Accept Terms: Read and agree to Terms of Service
  5. Create Account: Complete registration

Tips: Use a professional email, create a strong password (8+ characters), and keep credentials secure.

Completing Your Profile

For Professionals:

  • Personal Info: Full name, phone, location
  • Professional Details: Category, specialization, skills
  • Experience: Work history, education, certifications
  • Documents: NIN, certificates, CV, profile picture

For Institutions:

  • Organization: Name, contact details, location
  • Business Info: Type, description, industry

Why Complete Your Profile: Increases visibility, builds trust, and improves job matching.

Document Verification

Required Documents:

  • National ID (NIN) - Mandatory
  • Professional certificates/licenses
  • CV/Resume
  • Profile picture

Verification Process:

  1. Upload: Clear, readable documents (PDF, JPG, PNG, under 5MB)
  2. Review: Team reviews within 24-48 hours
  3. Status: Pending → Approved/Rejected
  4. Resubmit: If rejected, upload corrected documents

Platform Overview

Key Features:

  • Job Marketplace: Browse and apply for opportunities
  • Messaging: Direct communication between users
  • Verified Profiles: Document verification system
  • Notifications: Real-time updates
  • Ratings: Build reputation through reviews

Main Sections:

  • Dashboard: Activity overview
  • Find Jobs/Professionals: Search listings
  • Messages: Chat with other users
  • Profile: Manage your information
  • Settings: Account preferences

Finding and Applying for Jobs

How to Find Jobs:

  1. Navigate to "Find Jobs"
  2. Browse available listings
  3. Use filters: location, category, budget
  4. Click job to view full details

Applying:

  1. Review job requirements
  2. Click "Express Interest"
  3. Wait for institution to review your profile
  4. Get notified if accepted
  5. Chat opens automatically upon acceptance

Success Tips:

  • Complete your profile fully
  • Upload verified documents
  • Apply to matching jobs
  • Respond promptly to messages

Optimizing Your Profile

Profile Checklist:

  • Professional photo
  • Complete bio with detailed skills
  • Comprehensive experience section
  • All education and certifications listed
  • Verified documents uploaded
  • Current contact information

What Employers Look For:

  • Verified credentials
  • Relevant experience
  • Professional presentation
  • Complete information
  • Positive ratings

Uploading Documents

Steps:

  1. Go to Profile → Documents section
  2. Click "Upload Document"
  3. Select document type
  4. Choose file from device
  5. Add details if required
  6. Submit and wait for verification

Requirements:

  • Formats: PDF, JPG, PNG
  • Size: Maximum 5MB
  • Quality: Clear, readable, high resolution

Troubleshooting:

  • Check file size is under 5MB
  • Verify format is supported
  • Ensure stable internet connection
  • Try different browser if issues persist

Communicating with Institutions

Best Practices:

  • Respond Promptly: Within 24 hours
  • Be Professional: Proper grammar, respectful language
  • Ask Questions: Clarify job details, payment, expectations
  • Confirm Details: Start date, location, requirements

What to Discuss:

  • Job responsibilities and scope
  • Payment terms and schedule
  • Work location and hours
  • Required materials/equipment

Red Flags:

  • Requests for upfront payment
  • Vague job descriptions
  • Pressure to act immediately
  • Off-platform communication requests

How to Post a Job

Creating a Job:

  1. Go to Dashboard → "Post New Job"
  2. Fill in details:
    • Job title (clear, descriptive)
    • Category and specialization
    • Detailed description
    • Requirements and qualifications
    • Budget in UGX
    • Duration/deadline
    • Work location
  3. Review all information
  4. Publish job

Writing Effective Descriptions:

  • Be specific about needs
  • List all requirements
  • Set clear expectations
  • Include work hours, location, start date
  • Offer competitive compensation

Reviewing Applicants

Review Process:

  1. Check notifications for new interests
  2. View professional's full profile
  3. Verify uploaded documents
  4. Assess qualifications vs requirements
  5. Review experience and education
  6. Check ratings from previous jobs

What to Look For:

  • Verified documents (approved status)
  • Relevant skills matching job
  • Appropriate experience level
  • Complete profile information
  • Professional presentation
  • Proximity to job location

Making Decisions:

  • Accept: If qualified - chat opens automatically
  • Decline: If not a fit - professional notified
  • Compare: Review multiple candidates

Hiring a Professional

After Accepting Interest:

  1. Chat opens automatically
  2. Discuss job specifics
  3. Confirm arrangements
  4. Set start date
  5. Share necessary information

Important Discussions:

  • Payment: Total amount, schedule, method (Mobile Money, bank, cash)
  • Schedule: Start date/time, work hours, duration
  • Logistics: Exact location, materials needed, on-site contact

After Completion:

  • Verify work is satisfactory
  • Process payment as agreed
  • Leave rating and review
  • Close job posting

Managing Job Postings

Job Statuses:

  • Open: Accepting applications
  • Assigned: Professional accepted, work in progress
  • Completed: Work finished
  • Closed: No longer active

Available Actions:

  • Edit job details (before assignment)
  • Close job (stop accepting applications)
  • Reopen closed job
  • View all applicants
  • Delete job permanently

Changing Your Password

Steps:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Find Security/Password section
  3. Enter current password
  4. Enter new password (8+ characters)
  5. Confirm new password
  6. Save changes

Password Requirements:

  • Minimum 8 characters
  • Mix of uppercase and lowercase
  • Include numbers
  • Special characters recommended

Forgot Password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on login
  2. Enter email address
  3. Check email for reset link
  4. Create new password

Updating Email

How to Update:

  1. Navigate to Settings
  2. Find Email section
  3. Enter new email address
  4. Verify with current password
  5. Save changes
  6. Verify new email (check inbox)

Note: Use a professional email you have access to. Keep it current for important notifications.

Managing Notifications

Notification Types:

  • Job matches (new jobs for your profile)
  • Application updates
  • New messages
  • Interest acceptances
  • System announcements

Channels:

  • In-App: Bell icon in navigation
  • Email: Sent to registered email
  • Real-Time: Pop-ups when logged in

Managing Preferences:

Go to Settings → Notifications to toggle types on/off and adjust email frequency.

Deleting Account

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

What Gets Deleted:

  • Profile and personal information
  • All uploaded documents
  • Job postings (institutions)
  • Application history
  • Messages and conversations
  • Ratings and reviews

How to Delete:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Find "Delete Account" (usually at bottom)
  3. Read warning carefully
  4. Enter password to verify
  5. Confirm deletion

Alternatives: Consider deactivating temporarily or adjusting privacy settings instead.

Tips:

Message Notifications

How You're Notified:

  • Email notification
  • Sound alert (if enabled)

Manage preferences in Settings → Notifications.

Account Security

Protecting Your Account:

  • Strong Password: 8+ characters, mix of letters/numbers/symbols
  • Keep Private: Never share password
  • Secure Email: Use strong email password, enable 2FA

  • Update Software: Keep browsers current

If Compromised:

  1. Change password immediately
  2. Check account activity
  3. Contact support
  4. Review security settings

Avoiding Scams

Red Flags:

  • Requests for upfront payment
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers
  • Pressure to act immediately
  • Vague job descriptions
  • Off-platform communication requests
  • Requests for excessive personal info

Protect Yourself:

  • Verify users through profiles/documents
  • Stay on platform for communication
  • Trust your instincts
  • Research institution names
  • Report suspicious activity

If Scammed:

  1. Stop communication
  2. Report to QGig support
  3. Report to police if money lost
  4. Change passwords

Report Suspicious Activity

How to Report:

  1. Use report button on profiles/messages
  2. Email support@qgig.ug with:
    • User ID or profile link
    • Description of activity
    • Screenshots if available
    • Date and time

We investigate all reports and take appropriate action (warning, suspension, or ban).

Login Problems

Can't Log In?

Forgot Password:

  • Click "Forgot Password"
  • Enter email
  • Check email for reset link

Incorrect Credentials:

  • Check for typos
  • Verify Caps Lock is off
  • Try copying/pasting password

Account Locked:

  • Wait 15 minutes after multiple failed attempts
  • Use password reset if needed

Still Having Issues:

Contact support@qgig.ug with your email address.

Document Upload Problems

Payment Methods

Available Methods:

  • Mobile Money: MTN, Airtel (most common in Uganda)
  • Bank Transfer: Direct bank-to-bank
  • Cash: In-person payment

Note: Payment arrangements are made directly between professionals and institutions. QGig facilitates communication but doesn't process payments.

Best Practices:

  • Agree on payment terms before starting work
  • Use traceable payment methods
  • Get written confirmation
  • Keep payment records

Service Fees

QGig Fees:

  • Registration: Free for all users
  • Browsing: Free to browse jobs/professionals
  • Service Fee: Small fee on successful job completion

Specific fee structures will be communicated clearly before any charges apply.

Payment Disputes

If Payment Issues Arise:

  1. Communicate: Try to resolve directly with other party
  2. Document: Keep records of agreements and communications
  3. Contact Support: Email support@qgig.ug if unresolved
  4. Provide Evidence: Share relevant messages and agreements

Prevention: Always agree on payment terms in writing before starting work.

Invoices and Receipts

Getting Receipts:

For any QGig service fees charged, receipts will be sent to your registered email.

Between Users:

For payments between professionals and institutions, we recommend:

  • Request written receipt from payer
  • Include: date, amount, service description, names
  • Keep copies for your records
  • Use Mobile Money transaction records
