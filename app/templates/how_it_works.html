How QGig Works

Connecting skilled professionals with institutions across Uganda has never been easier

For Professionals

Showcase your skills, find opportunities, and grow your career. Whether you're a teacher, nurse, plumber, or electrician, QGig connects you with institutions looking for your expertise.

For Institutions

Find qualified professionals quickly and efficiently. Post jobs, review verified candidates, and hire the right talent for your organization with confidence.

For Professionals: Find Your Next Opportunity

Follow these simple steps to get started

1

Create Your Profile

Sign up and complete your professional profile. Add your skills, experience, education, and specialization. Upload your NIN, certificates, CV, and profile picture to build trust with potential employers.

2

Browse Job Opportunities

Explore available jobs posted by institutions across Uganda. Filter by location, category, budget, and requirements to find opportunities that match your skills and preferences.

3

Express Your Interest

Found a job you like? Click "Express Interest" to apply. Institutions will review your profile, skills, and uploaded documents to evaluate your qualifications.

4

Get Accepted & Start Chatting

When an institution accepts your interest, you'll receive a notification and the chat will automatically open. Discuss job details, negotiate terms, and finalize arrangements directly with the employer.

5

Complete the Job

Work with the institution, deliver quality service, and build your reputation. After completion, receive ratings and reviews that enhance your profile for future opportunities.

For Institutions: Hire Quality Talent

Post jobs and find the perfect professional

1

Register Your Institution

Create an account with your institution details. Provide your organization name, contact information, location, and a brief description of your business or organization.

2

Post a Job

Create detailed job postings with clear requirements, responsibilities, budget, and duration. Specify the skills and qualifications you're looking for to attract the right candidates.

3

Review Applicants

Receive notifications when professionals express interest. Review their profiles, check their verified documents, read about their experience, and evaluate their qualifications.

4

Accept & Communicate

Select the best candidate and accept their interest. A chat will automatically open, allowing you to discuss job details, clarify expectations, and finalize arrangements.

5

Work Together

Collaborate with the professional to complete the job. After completion, rate and review their performance to help other institutions make informed hiring decisions.
