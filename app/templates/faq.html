Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about QGig platform

General Questions

What is QGig?

QGig is Uganda's premier platform connecting skilled professionals with institutions and businesses. Whether you're a professional looking for gigs or an institution seeking qualified talent, QGig makes the connection seamless and secure.

Who can use QGig?

QGig is designed for two main user groups:
  • Professionals: Skilled workers in both formal and informal sectors (teachers, nurses, plumbers, electricians, etc.)
  • Institutions: Businesses, hospitals, schools, hotels, and organizations looking to hire professionals

Is QGig available throughout Uganda?

Yes! QGig operates across all regions of Uganda. Professionals and institutions from Kampala, Entebbe, Jinja, Mbarara, Gulu, and all other cities and towns can use our platform.

For Professionals

How do I register as a professional?

Registration is simple:
  • Click "Sign Up" and select "Professional"
  • Fill in your personal details and create a password
  • Complete your profile with skills, experience, and qualifications
  • Upload required documents (NIN, certificates, CV)
  • Start browsing and applying for jobs!

What documents do I need to upload?

Required documents include:
  • National Identification Number (NIN) card
  • Professional certificates or licenses (if applicable)
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Profile picture
These documents help institutions verify your credentials and build trust.

How do I apply for jobs?

Browse available jobs in the "Find Jobs" section, click on any job that interests you, and click "Express Interest". The institution will review your profile and documents, and if selected, they'll accept your interest and you can start chatting directly.

Is there a fee to use QGig as a professional?

Registration and browsing jobs is completely free. You only pay a small service fee when you successfully complete a job through the platform.

For Institutions

How do I post a job?

After registering as an institution:
  • Go to your dashboard and click "Post New Job"
  • Fill in job details (title, description, requirements, budget)
  • Set the job duration and location
  • Publish the job to start receiving applications

How do I review applicants?

When professionals express interest in your job, you'll receive notifications. You can view their profiles, check their documents, read their experience, and review their qualifications. Accept the best candidate to start working together.

How does payment work?

Payment terms are agreed upon between you and the professional. QGig facilitates secure communication and document verification but does not handle direct payments. You can arrange payment through Mobile Money, bank transfer, or cash as agreed.

Can I hire multiple professionals for one job?

Currently, each job posting is designed for one professional. If you need multiple workers, you can post separate jobs or contact our support team for bulk hiring assistance.

Account & Security

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes! We take data security seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties without your consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account anytime from the Settings page. Please note that this action is permanent and will remove all your data from our system.

Technical Support

I'm having trouble uploading documents. What should I do?

Ensure your documents are in PDF, JPG, or PNG format and under 5MB in size. If problems persist, try using a different browser or contact our support team at support@qgig.ug.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team through:
  • Email: support@qgig.ug
  • Phone: +256 700 000 000
  • Visit our Help Center for more resources
We typically respond within 24 hours.
